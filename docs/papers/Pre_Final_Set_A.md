## **Part A (20 Marks)**
**I. Choose the correct answer from the given choices. (1 x 15 = 15)**

1.  Write the set $A = \{x : x \in Z, x^2 < 4\}$ in roster form.
    (a) $\{-1, 0, 1\}$
    (b) $\{-2, -1, 0, 1, 2\}$
    (c) $\{0, 1\}$
    (d) $\{-1, 1\}$

2.  If $f(x) = x^2$ and $g(x) = 2x + 1$, then $(f+g)(2)$ is:
    (a) 5
    (b) 9
    (c) 4
    (d) 10

3.  The value of $\sin(765^\circ)$ is:
    (a) $1/\sqrt{2}$
    (b) $1/2$
    (c) $\sqrt{3}/2$
    (d) 1

4.  Multiplicative inverse of $4 - 3i$ is:
    (a) $\frac{4}{25} + i\frac{3}{25}$
    (b) $\frac{4}{25} - i\frac{3}{25}$
    (c) $4 + 3i$
    (d) $\frac{4-3i}{25}$

5.  The value of $^nC_n$ is:
    (a) $n!$
    (b) 1
    (c) 0
    (d) $n$

6.  The number of terms in the expansion of $(x + a)^n$ is:
    (a) $n$
    (b) $n+1$
    (c) $n-1$
    (d) $2n$

7.  The 10th term of the G.P. 5, 25, 125, ... is:
    (a) $5^9$
    (b) $5^{10}$
    (c) $5^{11}$
    (d) $5^8$

8.  Slope of the line passing through the points (3, -2) and (-1, 4) is:
    (a) -3/2
    (b) 3/2
    (c) -2/3
    (d) 2/3

9.  The equation of the circle with centre at (0, 2) and radius 2 is:
    (a) $x^2 + y^2 + 4y = 0$
    (b) $x^2 + y^2 - 4y = 0$
    (c) $x^2 + y^2 - 2y = 0$
    (d) $x^2 + y^2 + 2y = 0$

10. The distance of the point P(1, -3, 4) from the origin is:
    (a) $\sqrt{26}$
    (b) 26
    (c) $\sqrt{14}$
    (d) $\sqrt{12}$

11. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x}$ is equal to:
    (a) 0
    (b) 1
    (c) -1
    (d) undefined

12. The derivative of $\tan x$ is:
    (a) $\sec^2 x$
    (b) $\cot x$
    (c) $-\sec^2 x$
    (d) $\sec x \tan x$

13. Mean of first $n$ natural numbers is:
    (a) $\frac{n(n+1)}{2}$
    (b) $\frac{n+1}{2}$
    (c) $\frac{n}{2}$
    (d) $n+1$

14. If $P(E) = 0.6$, then $P(\text{not } E)$ is:
    (a) 0.6
    (b) 0.4
    (c) 0
    (d) 1

15. Two coins are tossed once. Probability of getting two heads is:
    (a) 1/2
    (b) 1/4
    (c) 3/4
    (d) 1/3

## **Part B (12 Marks)**
**Answer any SIX of the following questions. (2 x 6 = 12)**

21. If $A = \{1, 2, 3\}$ and $B = \{3, 4\}$, find $A \cup B$ and $A \cap B$.
22. Find the domain and range of the function $f(x) = \sqrt{9-x^2}$.
23. Find the value of $\tan 15^\circ$.
24. Solve the quadratic equation $x^2 + 3x + 9 = 0$.
25. Find the value of $n$ if $^nC_8 = ^nC_2$.
26. Find the coefficient of $x^5$ in the expansion of $(x+3)^8$.
27. Insert 3 numbers between 1 and 256 so that the resulting sequence is a G.P. 
28. Find the equation of the line perpendicular to $x - 2y + 3 = 0$ passing through (1, -2).
29. Find the coordinates of the focus and the equation of the directrix of the parabola $y^2 = -8x$.
30. Evaluate $\lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3 - 4x^2 + 4x}{x^2 - 4}$.
31. Two dice are thrown. Find the probability that the sum of the numbers appearing on them is 8.

## **Part C (18 Marks)**
**Answer any SIX of the following questions. (3 x 6 = 18)**

32. In a group of 400 people, 250 can speak Hindi and 200 can speak English. How many people can speak both Hindi and English?
33. Let $f = \{(1,1), (2,3), (0,-1), (-1,-3)\}$ be a linear function from Z to Z. Find $f(x)$.
34. Prove that $\frac{\cos 4x + \cos 3x + \cos 2x}{\sin 4x + \sin 3x + \sin 2x} = \cot 3x$.
35. Convert the complex number $\frac{-16}{1+i\sqrt{3}}$ into polar form.
36. Solve the system of inequalities graphically: $x + y \ge 5$, $x - y \le 3$.
37. Find the number of arrangements of the letters of the word INDEPENDENCE. In how many of these do the words start with P?
38. Find the term independent of $x$ in the expansion of $(\frac{3x^2}{2} - \frac{1}{3x})^6$.
39. The sum of first three terms of a G.P. is $13/12$ and their product is -1. Find the terms.
40. Find the equation of the ellipse whose vertices are $(\pm 5, 0)$ and foci are $(\pm 4, 0)$.
41. Find the derivative of $\tan x$ with respect to $x$ from first principles.
42. Find the mean deviation about the median for the data: 3, 9, 5, 3, 12, 10, 18, 4, 7, 19, 21.
