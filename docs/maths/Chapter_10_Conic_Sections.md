# Chapter 10: Conic Sections - Important Questions

## 1 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the centre and radius of the circle $x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 8y - 45 = 0$.
   **Answer:** Centre $(2, 4)$, Radius $\sqrt{4 + 16 - (-45)} = \sqrt{65}$.
2. **Question:** Find the focus of the parabola $y^2 = -8x$.
   **Answer:** $4a = 8 \implies a=2$. Focus $(-2, 0)$.

## 2 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the equation of the ellipse whose vertices are $(\pm 5, 0)$ and foci are $(\pm 4, 0)$.
   **Answer:** $a=5, c=4 \implies b^2 = 25 - 16 = 9$. Eq: $\frac{x^2}{25} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1$.

## 3 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the equation of the hyperbola with foci $(0, \pm 3)$ and vertices $(0, \pm \frac{\sqrt{11}}{2})$.
   **Answer:** Vertices on y-axis. $c=3, a=\frac{\sqrt{11}}{2}$. $b^2 = c^2 - a^2 = 9 - \frac{11}{4} = \frac{25}{4}$. Eq: $\frac{y^2}{11/4} - \frac{x^2}{25/4} = 1 \implies 4y^2/11 - 4x^2/25 = 1$.
