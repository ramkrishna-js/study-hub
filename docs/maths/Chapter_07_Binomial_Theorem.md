# Chapter 7: Binomial Theorem - Important Questions

## 1 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the number of terms in the expansion of $(x + \frac{1}{x})^{10}$.
   **Answer:** $10 + 1 = 11$ terms.

## 2 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the coefficient of $x^5$ in $(x + 3)^8$.
   **Answer:** $T_{r+1} = ^8C_r x^{8-r} 3^r$. Let $8-r=5 \implies r=3$. Coeff = $^8C_3 \times 3^3 = 56 \times 27 = 1512$.

## 3 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the term independent of $x$ in the expansion of $(\frac{3x^2}{2} - \frac{1}{3x})^6$.
   **Answer:** $T_{r+1} = ^6C_r (\frac{3x^2}{2})^{6-r} (-\frac{1}{3x})^r$. Power of $x$: $2(6-r) - r = 0 \implies 12 - 3r = 0 \implies r=4$.
   Term = $^6C_4 (\frac{3}{2})^2 (-\frac{1}{3})^4 = 15 \times \frac{9}{4} \times \frac{1}{81} = \frac{5}{12}$.

## 5 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** The coefficients of the $(r-1)^{th}$, $r^{th}$ and $(r+1)^{th}$ terms in the expansion of $(x+1)^n$ are in the ratio 1:3:5. Find $n$ and $r$.
   **Answer:** $^nC_{r-2} : ^nC_{r-1} : ^nC_r = 1:3:5$. Solve the system of equations to get $n=7, r=3$. (Verify: $^7C_1=7, ^7C_2=21, ^7C_3=35$ -> 1:3:5).
