# Chapter 11: Introduction to 3D Geometry - Important Questions

## 1 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the distance between points P(2, 3, 5) and Q(4, 3, 1).
   **Answer:** $\sqrt{(4-2)^2 + (3-3)^2 + (1-5)^2} = \sqrt{4 + 0 + 16} = \sqrt{20} = 2\sqrt{5}$.

## 2 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Show that the points (-2, 3, 5), (1, 2, 3) and (7, 0, -1) are collinear.
   **Answer:** Use distance formula ($AB + BC = AC$) or direction ratios.

## 3 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the ratio in which the YZ-plane divides the line segment formed by joining the points (-2, 4, 7) and (3, -5, 8).
   **Answer:** Let ratio be $k:1$. X-coordinate of dividing point is 0.
   $x = \frac{3k - 2}{k+1} = 0 \implies 3k = 2 \implies k = 2/3$. Ratio is 2:3.

## 5 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the coordinates of the centroid of the triangle with vertices $(x_1, y_1, z_1), (x_2, y_2, z_2), (x_3, y_3, z_3)$. Prove it.
   **Answer:** Derivation showing $(\frac{x_1+x_2+x_3}{3}, \frac{y_1+y_2+y_3}{3}, \frac{z_1+z_2+z_3}{3})$.
