## Question Paper Pattern (80 Marks)
- **Section A (1 Mark each):** 15 MCQs + 5 Fill in the blanks = 20 Marks
- **Section B (2 Marks each):** Answer 6 out of 11 = 12 Marks
- **Section C (3 Marks each):** Answer 6 out of 11 = 18 Marks
- **Section D (5 Marks each):** Answer 4 out of 8 = 20 Marks
- **Section E (Case-based/Long):** Answer 1 out of 2 = 10 Marks (often 6+4)

*Note: Principle of Mathematical Induction and Mathematical Reasoning are excluded as per recent NCERT rationalization.*
