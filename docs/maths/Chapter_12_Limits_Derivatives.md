# Chapter 12: Limits & Derivatives - Important Questions

## 1 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Evaluate $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin ax}{\sin bx}$.
   **Answer:** $a/b$.
2. **Question:** Find the derivative of $\sin x$ at $x=0$.
   **Answer:** $\cos(0) = 1$.

## 2 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Evaluate $\lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3 - 4x^2 + 4x}{x^2 - 4}$.
   **Answer:** $\lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x(x-2)^2}{(x-2)(x+2)} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x(x-2)}{x+2} = 0$.

## 3 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the derivative of $\tan x$ from first principles.
   **Answer:** Use $f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\tan(x+h) - \tan x}{h}$. (Standard derivation).
2. **Question:** Find the derivative of $y = \frac{x + \cos x}{\tan x}$.
   **Answer:** Use Quotient Rule.

## 5 Mark Questions
1. **Question:** Find the derivative of $f(x) = \sin x$ from first principles.
   **Answer:** Standard derivation using $\sin C - \sin D$ formula.
